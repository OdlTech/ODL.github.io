Procedure for Companies
1. The ODL office contacts different companies with relevant information.
2. The DL office appoints a representative as a point of contact (POC) for the interested companies.
3. Companies need to contact the concerned POC (or mail to odl_placement@iitk.ac.in ) to be
updated about the ongoing placement process.
4. Companies need to fill out a Job Announcement Form (JAF) to share the offered job-profile details.
ODL also encourages the lateral placement process. (For companies interested in lateral
placement, please reach out to odl_placement@iitk.ac.in).
5. After verification of details by POC, job openings are made available to the students as per dates
decided by the ODL.
6. Students need to submit their resumes through ODL before the company's deadline.
7. Companies conduct the pre-placement talks (PPT) and tests/ screening process, preferably in
virtual mode, after finalizing the schedule with the ODL team.
8. Companies submit the final list of shortlisted students for the interview process to the ODL.
9. Companies need to inform the dates of the interview to the ODL office over email.
10. Companies provide the list of selected and waitlisted students to the ODL within a day after the
completion of the selection process.
11. Companies are discouraged to send offer letters directly to the selected candidates. Companies'
offer letters should come to the ODL office, and the ODL office sends them to the respective
candidates.
12. The job offer should be consistent with the company's JAF.
13. The ODL notifies the companies regarding the acceptance of selected students.
