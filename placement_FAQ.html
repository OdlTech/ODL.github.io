Frequently Asked Questions for Career Support




The placement registration is NOT compulsory for all students. Only students who are seeking career opportunities and need the support of ODL should consider registering for placement.

No, ODL operates independently of SPO, as the placement processes differ. SPO works through Campus placement, while ODL follows a lateral placement approach due to the diverse backgrounds and varied work experiences of its students.

The process involves placement pre-registration and registration by the students, resume verification, company outreach, sharing student profiles, arranging interviews, and sharing final job offers. It is a student-driven process with different roles handled by eMasters students and the ODL placement team.

ODL team assesses students' eligibility for placement by ensuring that they are at their last quarter of the programme.
  • A student will be eligible for placement only if;
  • The student has completed nine or more modules successfully. AND
  • The student has registered for at least (12 - Y) modules in the placement quarter, where Y (>= 9) is the number of successfully completed modules by the student.
    • The registration is open for those who will potentially complete the programme in the next quarter. The registration for placement is open every quarter.

The ODL team requires a No Objection Certificate for any student sponsored/nominated by an organisation from the concerned authorities/agencies prior to being eligible for placement.

Registration is open every quarter, starting with placement pre-registration and student placement registration aligned with the Academic Pre-Registration. Pre-registration ends before the end-quarter exam, and student placement registration concludes one week after the end-quarter exam. The dates for both are available at the student portal.

Currently, there is NO placement registration fee for eMasters students. However, registration is mandatory to access placement services.

Interested students must upload a master resume and supporting documents for verification. They also submit a list of preferred companies for employment.

No, there is currently no set format or length or limit for the master resume. A student should add all the qualifications, experiences, skills and other relevant information which can enrich the CV as well as include all information of their profile.

Students upload a concise two-page resume which can be shared with companies. The 2 page resume should be a subset of the master resume.

In order to be considered for placement, students must complete both the "Pre-Placement Registration" and "Student Placement Registration" forms.
The "Placement Pre-Registration" is completely different from "Academic Pre-Registration".

Students can participate in placement services extended by ODL only once, within one year of their graduation from IIT Kanpur. The registration will be valid for one placement season.

Once a student completes both the pre-registration and student placement registration forms, the student does not have to register again. The registration will be considered from the quarter he becomes eligible for placement.

ODL circulates a form allowing registered students to choose whether to share their resume with companies directly or with prior permission, ensuring control over resume distribution. Also, ODL shares the job description with the students and only forwards the resume if the student shows interest in seeking that job.

ODL seeks support from students through roles like Department Point of Contact (DPOC), Industry Coordinator (IC), and Social Media Coordinator (SMC). Final selections are made in consultation with existing student POCs and PCs. The responsibility associated with these positions are highlighted in a supportive google form which is shared with students twice a year or before a new programme is due for placement preregistration.

ODL acts as a facilitator and counselor, not guaranteeing a job. The process duration varies, with some securing offers before completion, while others take months post-graduation or through independent sources.

ODL follows a one-student-one-job policy. Refer to the Placement Policy at the student portal for more details.

Currently, there is NO placement registration fee for eMasters students. However, registration is mandatory to access placement services.
