Programe Fees waiver

IIT Kanpur eMasters programme offers incentives in the form of discounts to employees of government, defense, PSU, MSME, and other corporate employees. The approved maximum waiver is listed below. However, the exact waiver is at the discretion of the concerned program coordinator in consultation with the HoD. The concerned entity (Department/PSU/MSME/ Corporate) must come to an agreement with IIT Kanpur or provide a formal/written document when sponsoring/nominating students for the eMasters Programme.


Table 1: Employees of Government, PSUs, or Defense Departments
S No Category Nominated/Sponsored* Max Applicable Fee Waiver
1 Defense Personnel Sponsored Fee waiver of last 3 modules
2 Defense Personnel Nominated Fee waiver of last 2 modules
3 PSU/Govt. Depts Sponsored Fee waiver of last 2 modules
4 PSU/Govt. Depts Nominated Fee waiver of last 1 module


The above waivers are applicable even if one candidate is nominated/sponsored by the concerned entities. Government includes constitutional bodies as well as non- constitutional bodies (i.e., all statutory, regulatory, executive bodies) like Finance Commission, UPSC, SEBI, TRAI, CERC/SERCs, RBI, FSSAI, NHB, Niti Ayog, CBI etc.
