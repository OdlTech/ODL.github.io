Highlights of our Campus Immersion Programme - Dept. of Enonomic Sciences

Posted on May 22, 2024

The Campus Immersion Programme (CIP) video showcases a vibrant collection of photos highlighting the recent gathering of students from the Department of Economic Sciences at IIT Kanpur. Students from the Business Finance and Public Policy (BFPP) and Climate Finance and Sustainability (CFS) programs participated in this enriching experience. The event featured meetings with program coordinators and faculty members, providing valuable insights into the eMasters program's workflow. Faculty guided students on selecting modules within their respective programs. The program also included lively dinners, fostering connections among students and faculty in a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere.
